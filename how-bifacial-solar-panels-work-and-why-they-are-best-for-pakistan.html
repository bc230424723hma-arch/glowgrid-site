How Bifacial Solar Panels Work & Why They Are Best for Pakistan

20255 min read

Bifacial solar panels generate electricity from both front and rear sides. This allows them to capture direct sunlight from the front and reflected light from surfaces below, increasing total output.

How They Work

Unlike traditional single-sided panels, bifacial modules use dual-glass/dual-side cell architecture to utilize reflected irradiance.

Why They Perform Well in Pakistan

  • High annual sunlight availability
  • Strong performance potential on reflective rooftops and open commercial sites
  • Better output opportunity in optimized mounting conditions

Expected Gain

Depending on design, height, tilt, albedo, and spacing, additional energy gains are often possible versus monofacial setups.

Where They Fit Best

Bifacial modules are useful in both residential and commercial systems, especially where structural design allows rear-side light collection.

Design Note: Bifacial gains depend heavily on mounting and site engineering — proper design is essential.

Conclusion

For Pakistan’s high-sunlight environment, bifacial technology can be an excellent long-term choice when paired with quality installation and correct structural planning.
