The Solar Journey
01 / Capture

Smart Solar Panels

Our Tier-1 monocrystalline bifacial panels from Jinko and Longi absorb sunlight with industry-leading efficiency, generating maximum DC power even in low-light conditions.

02 / Convert

Intelligent Inverters

Growatt, Huawei, and Sungrow inverters seamlessly convert DC energy into usable AC power, optimizing flow and preventing energy loss with 98%+ efficiency ratings.

03 / Store

Battery Backup

Excess daytime energy is stored in sleek, high-capacity lithium batteries. Use your stored solar power freely during the night or during load shedding.

04 / Share

Net-Metering Export

When your batteries are full, surplus power is exported to WAPDA grid via net metering. Your DISCO credits these units — watch your monthly bill drop to zero.

Which System Is Right for You?

On-Grid System

Best if you have stable grid. Maximum ROI with net metering. Feed excess electricity back and earn credits.

Hybrid System

Best if you face load shedding. Combines solar + battery storage for 24/7 power independence.

Off-Grid System

Best for remote areas with no grid. 100% battery-based, fully independent from WAPDA.
